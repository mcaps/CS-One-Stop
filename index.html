Dear Computer Science Student...

You've spent hours stuck in class learning about Finite State Automata while some 17 year old kid just made a couple million dollars after selling his iPhone app. Every job you've applied to asks to be fluent in a language you've never even heard of before, while you finish up your class project in Assembly. You know you need real world experience but don't even know where to begin looking for it. There's a huge divide between a CS degree and real-world skills. CS One Stop is the bridge between and the guiding light along your Computer Science career path.

What is CS One Stop?

CS One Stop is the go to place for Computer Science students, whether they are in high school, college, recently graduated or just simply interested in the computer science field. Here they will find everything from tutorials for popular programming languages, a compiler that will allow them to run code from any internet connected device, information on the lastest technology and news, career resources, such as what skills employers are looking for, and a course management system, where instructors and students can interact.
